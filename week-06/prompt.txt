2. UI GENERATION PROMPT:

Mario’s Bakery is warm, cozy, and artisanal, with a slightly premium feel. It should reflect comfort, freshness, and a welcoming atmosphere.

Young people and international students.

Design requirements:
- Hero section with a large image of fresh bread and pastries

- Clear call-to-action button: "View Menu"

Sections to include:
1. Featured baked goods
2. About the bakery (short description)
3. Menu preview

Visual style:
- Minimalist and clean layout
- Warm color palette (beige, brown, golden tones)
- Elegant typography (serif for headings, sans-serif for body text)

Mobile-first design.
