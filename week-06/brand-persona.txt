BRAND PERSONA: MARIO’S BAKERY

Personality / Tone:
Mario’s Bakery is a warm, cozy, and artisanal brand with a slightly premium feel. It conveys comfort, simplicity, and authenticity, like a local bakery where people can relax and enjoy freshly baked goods. The tone is friendly, inviting, and natural, avoiding anything too corporate or overly formal.

Color Palette:
- Primary color (dark brown): #3E2723
- Secondary color (warm beige): #F5E9DC
- Accent color (soft gold): #D4A373

Typography (Google Fonts):
- Headings: Playfair Display (serif, elegant and premium feel)
- Body text: Inter (clean, modern, and highly readable)

Marketing Pitch:
Mario’s Bakery is a cozy, artisanal bakery that brings people together through freshly baked goods and warm, simple moments. It offers a welcoming space where quality, comfort, and tradition meet.
