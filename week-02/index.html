ABOUT US

Mario’s Bakery began as a small idea in a quiet kitchen, where early mornings smelled of fresh dough and strong coffee. What started with a single oven and a handwritten notebook of family recipes slowly became a place where people gathered, not just for bread, but for a moment of pause in their day.
